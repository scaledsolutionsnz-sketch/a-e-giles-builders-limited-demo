About A E Giles Builders

Wānaka builders who know
what quality looks like.

A E Giles Builders Limited is a trusted Wānaka construction company led by Andy Giles. We've been delivering quality residential building projects across Wānaka, Hawea, and the wider Otago region for years — and we're as busy as ever because the work speaks for itself.

From premium new builds and architecturally designed homes to full renovations and extensions, our team brings the same high standard to every project. We're hands-on builders who care about the craft, the client, and the community we live and work in.

What We Build

Construction services for
every stage of your project.

Whether you're building your forever home on the shores of Lake Wānaka or renovating to make the most of what you've got — we have the skills and experience to deliver.

🏡
New Home Builds

Full residential builds from concept to completion. We work with your architect or designer to bring your vision to life — on time and to the quality Wānaka demands.

🔨
Renovations & Extensions

Transforming existing homes with thoughtful extensions, layout changes, and full renovations. We integrate new work seamlessly with the existing structure.

🏔️
Alpine & High-Spec Builds

Specialist construction for architecturally designed homes suited to the Wānaka climate — insulation, thermal performance, and premium finishes built to last.

🪵
Outdoor Living & Decks

Decking, outdoor entertaining areas, and landscaping structures that make the most of Wānaka's spectacular setting. Built tough, built beautiful.

📐
Project Management

End-to-end project management including consents, subcontractors, and timelines. We take the stress out of building so you don't have to manage it yourself.

🔧
Repairs & Maintenance

Weathertightness repairs, structural fixes, and general building maintenance. Honest, practical advice and solid workmanship you can rely on.
